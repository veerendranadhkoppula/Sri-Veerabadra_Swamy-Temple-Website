AboutTemple

Sri Badrakali Sametha Veerabhadra Swamy temple , the presiding deity of Yarramestti Vari Palem village in P. Gannavaram Mandal, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh, is a symbol of immense power and divine protection. Veerabhadra, born from Lord Shiva's wrath, is an embodiment of courage and fearlessness. Devotees revere him for his ability to safeguard them from harm and adversity. The temple, steeped in mythological significance, is a place of profound spiritual connection. Pilgrims visit to seek blessings, make offerings, and participate in rituals that celebrate the deity's strength. The awe-inspiring aura of Veerabhadra Swamy instills faith and determination in the hearts of those who worship here.

Swamy Kalyanam

Sri Badrakali Sametha Veerabadra Swamy Kalyanam is a grand annual event held in the last week of Magha Masam, specifically on Monday night, at veerabadra swamy temple. This auspicious ceremony celebrates the divine union of Sri Badrakali and Veerabadra Swamy. Devotees gather to witness the magnificent rituals, accompanied by vibrant processions and elaborate decorations. The temple comes alive with. fervent prayers and devotion, creating an atmosphere of spiritual bliss. This event exemplifies the rich cultural and religious traditions, bringing people together in the spirit of faith and unity. Sri Badrakali Sametha Veerabadra Swamy Kalyanam is a cherished and spiritually enriching occasion

Temple Festivals

"వీరభద్రస్వామి సంబరం"


Veerabhadra Sambaralu at the Veerabhadra Swamy Temple are annual celebrations in honor of Lord Veerabhadra, a fierce incarnation of Lord Shiva. Devotees gather to witness cultural programs, processions, and special rituals during this grand event, strengthening their devotion and seeking blessings for protection and prosperity.





"కార్తిక మాసం"


Karthika Masam is a sacred month in the Hindu lunar calendar, celebrated with devotion at Veerabhadra Swamy Temple. Devotees flock to offer special prayers, light lamps, and seek the blessings of Lord Veerabhadra. The temple's sanctity and spiritual atmosphere make it a significant pilgrimage destination during this auspicious month.


"మహా శివరాత్రి"


Maha Shivaratri is celebrated with great fervor at the Veerabhadra Swamy Temple in yarramsettivari palem. Devotees gather to offer prayers and seek the blessings of Lord Shiva. Special rituals, including abhishekam and night-long vigils, are conducted to honor the occasion, creating a spiritually charged atmosphere..
